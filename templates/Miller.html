Jarrell Miller

Jarrell King Miller is an American professional boxer and former kickboxer who competes in the Heavyweight division. He first came to prominence in 2007 when he competed for the New Jersey Tigers in the World Combat League and made it to the finals of the New York Golden Gloves tournament that same year. Miller is currently ranked the world's 4th best active heavyweight by Boxrec, and 8th by 'The Ring' magazine.
