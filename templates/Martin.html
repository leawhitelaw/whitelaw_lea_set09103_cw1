Charles Martin

Charles Lee Martin is an American professional boxer who held the IBF (International Boxing Federation) heavyweight title from January to April 2016. His 85-day reign as champion is the second-shortest in heavyweight boxing history, after Tony Tucker's 64 days in 1987 (also as IBF champion). During Martin's short reign as IBF champion, he reached a peak active heavyweight ranking of 9th by the Transnational Boxing Rankings Board.
